EN 207 / 208 Compliance

1. CE Marking: Ensure PPE bears the CE mark under EU Regulation 2016/425 on personal protective equipment.

2. LSO Certification: This tool maps exactly to standard EN207 Table 9-5. Final selection must be ratified by a certified Laser Safety Officer (LSO) per Directive 2006/25/EC.

3. Thermal Correction: The algorithm correctly applies thermal thresholds for high PRR, ensuring the resultant LB reflects both peak energy and average power demands.

4. EN 208 Limit: Alignment ratings (R) are valid solely for the visible spectrum (400-700 nm).
